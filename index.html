Welcome to BSides Munich

BSidesMUC is still in it's early stage, so there isn't much to see yet. Once it rolls, it will be a free infosec conference held in Munich. BSides are hosted all over the world and are community driven conferences, that are usually free to attend. This also means that the conference will be what every attendee makes of it. So if you go there why not give a talk, create a workshop, design a challenge for other attendees, help with the CTF, organization or sponsoring.

Newsletter: Sign up for our newsletter to receive updates about this conference, call-for-papers (CFP) and ticket availability.

Join us at April 3rd '17

BSides is happening in 2017 for the first time in Munich. We've set a date, found a venue but we're still in the middle of planning. Here's everything you can rely on so far.

  • Date: 3rd of April, 2017 in Munich - so save the date (3.4.17)
  • Time: Doors open at 09:00, the closing time is not yet final but will probably be between 17:00 - 19:00
  • Venue: Freibadstraße 30, 81543 München
  • Tickets: Not available yet, but will be gone fast once they're out
  • CFP: Call for Papers hasn't opened yet.

Rooms: We got one giant room for talks, and two smaller ones for workshops and such, so feel free to contact us if you have suggestions for CTF/LockPickVillage/Workshops and fun things like that.

Want to help?

If you want to help, just contact us. We need all the hands we can get. Tweet us @BsidesMunich, write us a mail to info at bsidesmunich dot org.
